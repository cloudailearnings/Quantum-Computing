QEC—Bit Flip Channel and Phase Flip Channel 

- 🎯Goal: 
Detect and correct quantum errors (like bit flip and phase flip) by using extra qubits and entanglement

- 🧪Syndrome measurement :
 - Extra ancilla qubits + CNOTs are used to extract an error pattern called syndrome. 

 - 00 → no error 
 - 01 → error on 3rd qubit 
 - 10 → error on 1st qubit 
 - 11 → error on 2nd qubit.
