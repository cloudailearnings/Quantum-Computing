About the job


We’re Hiring: Training Center Manager – DDU-GKY (Residential Skill Development Program)


 Location: Nandigama (Near Ambarupeta), NTR District, Andhra Pradesh
 Organization: KESTS Skill Development Center | KEATS Trust


About the Organization


KESTS Skill Development Center is operated by KEATS – Kakinada Engineering Alumni Trust, a reputed not-for-profit organization working in the areas of education, skilling, and empowerment.


Our residential training center at Ambarupeta near Nandigama implements DDU-GKY (Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana) programs with a strong focus on compliance, quality training delivery, placements, and trainee welfare.


Position Overview


We are seeking an experienced and committed Training Center Manager to lead the end-to-end operations of our DDU-GKY residential skill development center.


Only candidates willing to relocate to Nandigama (NTR District, Andhra Pradesh) need apply.


The ideal candidate must have a minimum of 5 years of proven experience in DDU-GKY or similar Government-sponsored skilling schemes, with hands-on exposure to residential, hostel, and dining (mess) operations.


Key Responsibilities


Manage overall operations of the DDU-GKY residential training center
Ensure full compliance with DDU-GKY guidelines, SOPs, inspections, audits, and MIS reporting
Plan and coordinate training schedules, assessments, certification, and placements
Supervise trainers, wardens, mobilization staff, kitchen, and support personnel
Oversee hostel and dining (mess) operations, ensuring food quality, hygiene, and safety standards
Coordinate with SRLM, PIAs, Government officials, assessors, and placement partners
Track training outcomes, placements, and post-placement follow-ups




Eligibility & Experience


Minimum 5 years of direct experience in DDU-GKY implementation (mandatory)
Experience managing residential skill development / training centers
Proven ability to manage hostel and mess/dining operations
Strong leadership, organizational, and people-management skills
Excellent communication and stakeholder-coordination abilities
Bachelor’s degree in Education, Business Management, Social Work, or related field (preferred)


Why Join KEATS / KESTS?


Leadership role in a mission-driven and credible trust
Opportunity to make direct social impact
Hands-on operational ownership in a residential setup
