Quantum Challenge: Ultra-Simple Basics! 🚀
Quick revision of qubit math—super easy bullets for fast learning.

•Bra-Ket: |ψ⟩ = mix of |0⟩ and |1⟩. Bra ⟨ψ| is its flip.

•Bit vs Qubit: Bit picks one. Qubit does both.

•Superposition: Qubit in “both” states at once.

hashtag#EasyMath
•Tensor: Joins qubits: |0⟩ ⊗ |1⟩ = |01⟩.

•Trace: Add diagonals, like 1+1=2.

•Pauli X/Y/Z flip and spin qubits!
